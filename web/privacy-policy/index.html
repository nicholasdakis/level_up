Level Up!

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Level Up! is a health and fitness gamification app made by Nicholas Dakis, a solo developer. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and what rights you have over it. No walls of legalese — just plain English.

What we collect

  • Account info Your email address, hashed password (never visible in plain text), and basic Google account info if you sign in with Google.
  • Profile Your chosen username and profile picture, stored on our servers.
  • Food logs Food names, macros, calories, serving sizes, and the dates you logged them.
  • Nutrition goals Your calorie, protein, carbs, fat, and weight goal preferences.
  • Calorie Calculator inputs Age, height, weight, sex, and activity level. Used only for the in-app calculation — not stored on our servers.
  • Game progress Your XP, level, daily login streak, and achievement progress.
  • Location GPS coordinates while the Explore tab is open, used to find nearby points of interest and confirm check-ins. Never accessed in the background.
  • Explore history Which points of interest you have visited and when.
  • Reminders The messages and scheduled times you set for reminders.
  • Push notification tokens A device token (FCM) stored per device so reminders and notifications can be delivered to you.
  • App preferences Your chosen theme color and timezone offset.
  • Usage data Page views and in-app events collected via Firebase Analytics and Google Analytics (GA4).

How we use it

  • Run the app Keep your data synced across devices.
  • Gamification Calculate your XP, level, streaks, and achievement progress.
  • Reminders Send push notifications at the times you schedule.
  • Leaderboard Your username, level, XP, and profile picture are visible to other users.
  • Explore Find nearby points of interest and verify you are close enough to check in.
  • Improvement Understand how the app is used so it can be made better.

Third parties

  • Firebase / Google Authentication, push notifications, and analytics.
  • Supabase Database storage for your account and app data.
  • Render Hosts the backend server that processes your requests.
  • Upstash Redis cache used to speed up food search results.
  • FatSecret API Food search queries are sent to FatSecret to return nutritional data.
  • OpenFoodFacts API Barcode scans are sent to OpenFoodFacts to look up product information.

No data is sold to advertisers or any other third party.

Data retention

Your data is kept for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is removed from our servers. Anonymized or aggregated analytics data may be retained by Firebase and Google Analytics per their own policies.

Your rights

  • Edit your profile Update your username, profile picture, and goals at any time from the app.
  • Delete entries Remove individual food logs and reminders from within the app.
  • Delete your account To delete your account and all associated data, email n1ch0lasd4k1s@gmail.com with a deletion request. This is the only way to delete your account.
