How to delete your account

To permanently delete your Level Up! account and all associated data, send an email to n1ch0lasd4k1s@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" from the email address associated with your account.

Your account and all associated data — including your food logs, progress, and profile — will be permanently deleted within 7 days of your request.

Deletion is handled manually to verify your identity and prevent your account from being deleted without your knowledge.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All of your data will be removed, including your level, XP, achievements, food logs, reminders, and profile information.

Questions

If you have any questions about the deletion process, reach out to Nicholas Dakis at n1ch0lasd4k1s@gmail.com.
